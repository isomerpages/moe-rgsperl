<p>We are excited to announce that RGS has signed the 3rd Letter of Intent
(LOI) with the Centre for Research in Pedagogy and Practice (CRPP), National
Institute of Education (NIE).</p>
<p>This strategic partnership aligns our shared vision to advance educational
research that informs practice and policy, enhance professional learning,
and promote pedagogical innovations.</p>
<p>This Partnership will facilitate joint initiatives between CRPP and Centre
for Pedagogical Research and Learning that include PeRL Symposium and PED
Talks as well as joint research projects and publications. The aim is to
deepen teacher professionalism and practice within the school and beyond,
through diverse knowledge sharing platforms.</p>
<p>Together, we aspire to strengthen the educational community and inspire
impactful teaching and learning practices.</p>
